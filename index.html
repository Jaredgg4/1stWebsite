Overview

Beltline Auto Body & Repair has been a trusted name in the Irving, Texas area for several years. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction sets us apart. With a legacy of quality service, we cater to all your auto body needs. Our state-of-the-art facility boasts impressive features, including:

  • Two Paint Rooms: Ensuring precision and a flawless finish.
  • Two Frame Machines: Expertly handling structural repairs.
  • Hunter 611 Computerized Wheel Alignment System: Ensuring your vehicle’s alignment is spot-on.
  • Kansas Jack Computerized Measuring System: Accurate measurements for precise repairs.
  • DuPont Computerized Paint Mixing System: Delivering vibrant and durable paint finishes.
  • ICAR Certified Technicians: Skilled professionals dedicated to your vehicle’s well-being.

At Beltline Auto Body & Repair, we prioritize our customers, and their satisfaction is our driving force. Our loyal clientele consistently recommends us to friends and family. Trust us with your automotive needs—we’re here to serve you!
